About Bombdiggity

Introducing BombDiggity, a powerhouse 8-piece cover band based in Gent, Belgium. Despite being formed in February 2023, our collective experience and chemistry make us sound like a band that has been playing together for years.

Unforgetable Experience

Our band consists of Annelien Matthys and Indira Bergmans as our lead vocalists, Lajos Tauber and Kobe Walleghem on guitars, Tom Eylenbosch on keys, Wolf d'Anvers on bass, Sybe Versluys on drums and Stef Audenaert as our sound engineer/manager.

Whole Night Party

We take our profession seriously, and our meticulous attention to detail sets us apart from other cover bands. Our debut gig at the iconic Belgian venue, VierNulVier de Vooruit, was a smashing success. Since then, Bombdiggity has been captivating audiences with our infectious blend of 80's covers, danceable music, and a hint of rock influence. Our setlist includes songs from Spandau Ballet, Bon Jovi, Whitney Houston, and many other popular 80's artists, ensuring that everyone has a chance to sing and dance along. But don't just take our word for it. Book BombDiggity for your next event and experience the magic for yourself. We promise to bring the house down and leave your guests wanting more!
